Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-12

This Privacy Policy explains how this game (“Game”, “we”, “us”) collects and uses information when you play the Game on the Poki platform.

1. Information We Collect

We do not ask players to provide direct personal identifiers such as name, email address, phone number, or account credentials.

When you play the Game, we may collect technical and gameplay-related data, including gameplay analytics, technical information, and online identifiers used for analytics/configuration purposes.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

The collected data is used to analyze gameplay behavior, improve the Game, identify bugs, and evaluate gameplay changes.

3. A/B Testing and Remote Configuration

The Game may use A/B testing and remote configuration (feature flags) to enable/disable features, test variations, and roll out changes. These rely on anonymous data only.

4. Third-Party Services (GameAnalytics)

The Game uses GameAnalytics (GameAnalytics ApS) to process gameplay analytics, A/B testing, and remote configuration data.

Information about GameAnalytics’ privacy practices: https://www.gameanalytics.com/trust/privacy-notice

5. Cookies and Local Storage

The Game and embedded services may use cookies or browser local storage necessary for functionality, analytics, and configuration delivery.

6. GDPR (EEA)

If you are located in the EEA, processing is based on our legitimate interest in improving and maintaining the Game.

7. Children’s Privacy (COPPA)

The Game does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe such data was collected, contact us to remove it.

8. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes described above and may thereafter be deleted or aggregated.
